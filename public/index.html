What it is

Socialbridge is built by a small independent team with a focus on user safety and a smooth on-chain experience. Keys stay on-device and are protected with an encrypted vault-style architecture. We do not custody user funds.

Key features

  • Non-custodial: user keys stay under user control
  • Encrypted vault architecture for sensitive data
  • Multi-account support
  • TON Connect for dApps
  • Jettons & NFTs support
  • Built-in DeFi flows (swaps, liquidity, staking)

Fiat on-ramp / off-ramp

We plan to integrate third-party fiat on-ramp/off-ramp providers so users can buy and sell crypto directly from the wallet UI. KYC/AML and payment processing are handled by the provider.

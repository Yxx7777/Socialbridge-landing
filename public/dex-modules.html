DEX Modules Program

We are modularizing DeFi integrations so every swap, pool, or staking flow runs through the same safe, repeatable pipeline.

The team is building headless adapters for STON.fi, DeDust, and SwapCoffee. Each adapter outputs structured TxIntent[] objects, while the wallet remains responsible for sandbox emulation, risk gates, and sending the final transaction.

This separation keeps wallet UX familiar and lets us deploy fixes or improvements to one DEX without touching the others. If a provider goes offline, the wallet can fall back automatically and keep your flow moving.

Users will soon be able to pick their preferred DEX per swap, pool, or staking session, all from the same secure screen.

User benefits

  • Consistent safety checks and simulations across every DEX
  • Faster updates with independent rollout per module
  • Freedom to choose the venue you trust, plus automatic fallback

Already Delivered

A quick look at the features that are live in the Socialbridge wallet today.

Security-first vault

Socialbridge keeps private keys on your device inside an encrypted vault, so only you can authorize access. Nothing ever leaves your hardware unless you explicitly export an encrypted backup.

  • End-to-end on-device storage with secure enclave support
  • Vault unlock secured with hardware-backed PINs
  • Non-custodial by design: we never see seeds or passphrases

TON Connect + multi-account

Connect to TON dApps in one tap and keep multiple profiles ready for swaps, collections, or testing. Each account carries its own limits, jettons, and NFTs.

  • Native TON Connect flow without manual address sharing
  • Fast account switching with per-profile preferences
  • Unified asset view spanning TON, jettons, and collectibles

Built-in DeFi flows

Run swaps, add liquidity, or stake without leaving the wallet. Every flow is emulated via sandbox/TON API before you hit send, so outcomes are clear.

  • Preview final amounts, fees, and pool deltas before execution
  • Automated risk gates flag slippage or suspicious params
  • One approval to broadcast the verified transaction
